FAQs About TeraBox for iOS

What is TeraBox? +
TeraBox is a cloud storage service that provides large space for storing files, photos, and videos securely.
Is TeraBox available for iOS devices? +
Yes, TeraBox is available for both iOS and Android devices.
How much storage space does TeraBox offer on iOS? +
TeraBox offers 1TB of free storage space for all users, including those on iOS.
How do I protect my files on TeraBox? +
You can enable encryption and use security measures like password protection to keep your files safe on TeraBox.
